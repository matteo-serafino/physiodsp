# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for all. We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not tolerated:
- Harassment or discrimination based on identity
- Threats, intimidation, or abuse
- Spam or trolling
- Sexual harassment
- Doxing or sharing private information without consent

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers via email or private GitHub message.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from the community.
